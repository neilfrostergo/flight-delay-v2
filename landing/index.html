How it works

Three simple steps

Register once through your insurer's portal, and we handle everything from monitoring through to payment — automatically.

1

Validate your policy

Enter your policy number and email. We verify your cover and confirm the payout amount you're entitled to.

2

Register your flights

Search by flight number or route. Add all the flights you want to protect — as many as your policy allows.

3

Receive your payment

If a qualifying delay is confirmed, compensation lands in your bank via Faster Payments — typically within two hours.
